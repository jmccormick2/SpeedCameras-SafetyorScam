Chicago Speed Cameras: Safety or Scam?

By. James McCormick

Background

Since their inception in 2013, automated speed cameras have been a very controversial topic in Chicago. Proponents of the cameras hope for a detterent and reduction of speeding related fatalities, specifically around parks and schools. However, the opposition states that they are a tool of corrupt politics with many claims of unfairly victimizing already impoverished indiviuals. In this project, I intend to see if speed cameras do reduce the amount of vehicular crashes resulting in fatalities or if they do not affect public safety and are just an income source for the city.

Vehicular Fatalities

Although there are many causes of vehicular related fatalities, speeding could be a factor in these unecessary deaths. The dataset used shows the locations of vehicular fatalities that occured from 1/3/2019 to 9/21/2023 last being updated 10/18/2023. For the purpose of my data a "traffic fatality" is defined as "any death caused by a traffic crash, within 30 days of the crash" and "involves a motor vehicle". A victim can be a cyclist, driver, motorcyclist, passenger, and or pedestrian.

Findings

After creating these maps and have found no credible evidence of speed cameras preventing vehicular fatalities.Some areas surrond the camera locations do show a increase in fatalities, but I believe this is caused by outside factors, such as poor design or the busyness of an intersection. As previously mention there are many factors that can contribute to vehicular fatalities besides speeding. However in my opinon I think speed cameras have generated more revenue for the city than increasing and improving public safety.
